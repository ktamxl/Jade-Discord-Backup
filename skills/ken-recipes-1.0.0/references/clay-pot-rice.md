# Ultimate Clay Pot Rice 煲仔飯

**Cuisine:** Chinese — Cantonese Classic
**Serves:** 3–4
**Base Quantity:** 3 cups rice
**Rice Prep:** Soak 30–60 min + 1 hr fridge
**Cook Time:** ~35 min active
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

### The Rice Base
- 台山丝苗米 (Taishan Simiao Rice) — 3 cups
- Water — 2¾ cups
- Sesame oil (to coat pot) — ~1 tbsp

**Water ratio:** 1:1 baseline minus ¼ cup = 2¾ cups per 3 cups rice

### Toppings
- Chicken thigh, bite-sized chunks — 400–500g
- Lap Cheong (Chinese sausage), sliced diagonally — 2 links
- Dried shiitake mushrooms, rehydrated & sliced — 6–8 pieces
- Fresh ginger, thinly sliced — 6–8 slices

### Chicken Marinade
- Light soy sauce — 1½ tbsp
- Oyster sauce — 1 tbsp
- Sesame oil — 1 tsp
- Cornstarch — 1 tsp
- Sugar — ½ tsp
- White pepper — pinch
- Shaoxing wine — 1 tbsp

### For Crust & Finish
- Cooking oil (drizzled around lid edge) — 2–3 tbsp
- Lee Kum Kee Sweet Soy Sauce for Clay Pot Rice — to taste

---

## Instructions

1. **Wash, Soak & Refrigerate Rice** — Rinse 3–4 times, scrubbing gently between palms. Final rinse: keep water, soak 30–60 min. Strain, refrigerate uncovered 1 hour to dry.
2. **Marinate Toppings** — Combine chicken with marinade. Add shiitake, lap cheong, ginger. Toss. Marinate 30 min minimum.
3. **Oil the Pot** — Brush ~1 tbsp sesame oil over entire interior of dry clay pot.
4. **Add Rice & Water** — Add refrigerated rice, pour 2¾ cups cold water, level with gentle stir.
5. **High heat, uncovered** — Place on stove, high heat, NO lid, stay close with chopstick ready.
6. **First stir at the boil** — The moment water boils, stir gently to prevent sticking on bottom.
7. **Poke holes & add toppings (act fast!)** — When water nearly dry: poke holes with blunt end of chopstick, add toppings evenly.
8. **Cover, lower to low-medium, drizzle oil around lid edge** — Set timer 6 minutes.
9. **Tilt & rotate sequence on wok ring** — Left 2 min → Right 2 min → Front 2 min → Back 2 min (1 min pause between each).
10. **Pot flat, stove off, soy sauce around lid** — Turn stove off completely. Pour Lee Kum Kee Sweet Soy Sauce around lid edge. Do NOT open lid. Rest 8 minutes.
11. **Open, season, serve** — Open lid, drizzle more soy sauce to taste, stir, serve. Scrape the golden 鍋巴 crust from sides and bottom.

---

## Notes
- Only use 台山丝苗米 — non-negotiable
- Fridge step removes unpredictable surface moisture; makes water ratio reliable
- Heat level (step 8) is stove-specific — calibrate on first run
- Wok ring is essential for the tilt rotation sequence
- Lap cheong renders fat into rice — it's a seasoning agent, not just a topping
- Variations: salted fish + pork belly, frog legs, or crack an egg before covering (step 10)

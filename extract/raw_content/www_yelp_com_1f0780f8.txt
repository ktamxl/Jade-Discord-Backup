I have to say this is my go-to market. It's not a huge market. However, you can literally find anything in this store. They offer many different varieties of ingredients and spices. Not to say they're the only store in San Francisco that carry Koon Yick Curry Powder. I have to say that's one of the best curry powder ever. They also sell the best vermicelli noodle. The vermicelli they carry doesn't break apart easily or become mushy when boiled for too long. The texture is chewy and great for absorbing sauces. If you can find the ingredients you needed for your recipes. Definitely come check this place out. They speak English and different other Chinese dialogue.

I came here looking for ingredients for a Korean dish - I know, kind of counterintuitive to look in Chinatown for it, but even though the storekeeper was already closing shop, he was helpful in helping me find ingredients! Even with my mangled Chinese..I'm always scared to shop in Chinatown because I don't cook/buy Asian food enough to know if the prices were good, but I'm pretty happy with the prices at this store. $6 for a 5 pound bag of black rice - supa good deal! The sesame cooking oil might have been overpriced, but I just used it to stirfry and my dish tastes AMAZING. And..it was still only $2.40 so it couldn't have been that bad!

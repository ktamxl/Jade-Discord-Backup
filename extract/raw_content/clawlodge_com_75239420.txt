OpenClaw Workspace — Folder Structure
=====================================

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-21  

**Purpose:** Organized workspace for Oleg Zharsky's business operations

---

📁 Folder Overview
-----------------

### 01-Core/

System files, identity, and configuration

* **identity/** — AGENTS.md, IDENTITY.md, SOUL.md, USER.md, etc.
* **config/** — HEARTBEAT.md, TIMEZONE.md, TOOLS.md
* **system/** — OpenClaw system files, skills, scripts

### 02-Etsy-Shop/

Everything related to OZ3DPrint Etsy business

* **listings/** — Product copy, titles, tags, descriptions
* **strategies/** — Growth plans, pricing strategies
* **competitor-research/** — Market analysis, competitor intel
* **marketing/** — Promotional content, campaigns

### 03-Photography-Business/

Photography services and client work

* **quotes/** — Client quotes, proposals, pricing
* **client-materials/** — NFC cards, setup guides
* **growth-opportunities/** — Grants, publications, events

### 04-Marketing-Tools/

Lead generation and marketing assets

* **calculator/** — Nicotine Pouch Savings Calculator
* **landing-pages/** — HTML landing pages
* **email-sequences/** — Email automation flows

### 05-Strategy-Docs/

High-level business strategy

* **b2b-wholesale/** — Wholesale pricing, retailer targets
* **intelligence-reports/** — Competitor analysis, market research

### 06-Archive/

Old versions and drafts

* **old-versions/** — Previous iterations
* **drafts/** — Work in progress

### memory/

Daily logs and session notes

---

🔄 Auto-Sync to Google Drive
---------------------------

This workspace syncs to Google Drive every 8 hours automatically.

**Manual sync:**

```
./01-Core/system/sync-to-gdrive.sh

```

**Google Drive location:** `Gdrive:OpenClaw-Workspace/`

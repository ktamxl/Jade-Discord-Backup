# Why isn't my DM going through?
![](https://support.discord.com/system/photos/1500300735082/ZD\_Avatar\_the\_Last\_AirBender\_\_2\_.jpg)
[Bubbles](/hc/en-us/profiles/1504238994441-Bubbles)
\* 10 days ago
\* Updated
FollowNot yet followed by anyone
If your direct message was unable to be sent, you'll receive an error message from Clyde that states the following:
> \*\*Your message could not be delivered. This is usually because you don't share a server with the recipient or the recipient is only accepting direct messages from friends.\*\*
Clyde’s message will look something like this:
\*\*![](/hc/article\_attachments/38971509168663)\*\*
# \*\*Possible Reasons for This Error\*\*
\*\*There can be a few reasons why you are unable to send a direct message to someone.\*\* Here are possible reasons for the error that you may be encountering:
\* You don't share a [server](/hc/en-us/articles/360034842871) with the DM recipient.
\* You've [disabled direct messages](/hc/en-us/articles/217916488) on your shared server.
\* You have not completed the required [membership screening](/hc/en-us/articles/1500000466882) steps in your shared server.
\* The DM recipient is [only accepting direct messages from friends](/hc/en-us/articles/217916488).
\* You were [blocked](/hc/en-us/articles/217916488) by the DM recipient.
\* This error may also appear if the content you're trying to share has been identified as violating Discord's Community Guidelines and cannot be uploaded
If it seems you are running into a technical error when trying to send a DM, please reach out to our [Discord Support team](href). Otherwise, the Discord Support team will not be able to provide further assistance.
For further information, please review our [Blocking & Privacy article](/hc/en-us/articles/217916488) to learn more about these features.

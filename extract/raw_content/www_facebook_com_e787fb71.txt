懶人芝麻醬油排骨
Soy sauce chicken with sesame (Lazy girl style)
![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/t5f/1/16/1f4f9.png) reference: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t1SzAHql6BQ](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fwatch%3Fv%3Dt1SzAHql6BQ&h=AT5ZW7kg2pRofCs0-Ga2sAa6Atou3kImNA7Sf8h91NwFcbvq73IPwcH0wiX4XOIg\_RYvGIr8nQ5a217Zw7iqGEu1k5sSdDrlPk7NTxPpZ8FqWPBs1D16pMmhGdedy89cGa\_uW2UhqrFUHkwp0afIRw18zNfiVw0&\_\_tn\_\_=-UK-R&c[0]=AT686Zbu8r7zpiOihaXYUbN9mNOo-dWhY8vzAkjWf22fPNmCcXLB62dCfqj7WqfcHsX8JfWUYnBjNUQKDyHq8VkOyvwzArxF\_g6sjfvWxs2EXbiZWYixo3jq4qQQ3LTN-kzLU3CDLWWGOmDAeJ0s6tZcYd4jPXtXPFY0UlUffUag-sccGmhBTDscevi9)

材料
- 排骨 pork bones
- 糖 2勺 white sugar 2tsp
- 米酒1勺 1 tsp cooking wine
- 生抽2勺 2 tsp light soy sauce
- 老抽1勺 1 tsp dark soy sauce
- 醋 1勺 2tsp vinegar
- 蔥薑蒜 chopped ginger, green onion root, garlic
- 1杯水 1 cup of water
做法
1. 排骨汆燙, 撈起 pork bones in water, bring to boil then get rid of the water
2. 將所有的材料一併加入鍋內, 中火煮至收汁即可上桌 add all the ingredients into a pan, medium heat till water evaporates
3. 加入白芝麻點綴一下 add water saseme on top of it ot serve

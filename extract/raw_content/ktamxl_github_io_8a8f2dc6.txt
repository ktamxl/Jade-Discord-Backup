📋 Good to Know
--------------

💳
Cashless Payments
Download **Alipay** and **WeChat Pay** before arriving in Shanghai and Shenzhen. Most places don't take foreign cards — these apps are essential. Link to a foreign card or bring cash to top up.

🚇
Getting Around
Use **DiDi** for ride-hailing in Shanghai & Shenzhen (works like Uber). For Shanghai subway, use the **Shanghai Metro app** or **MetroMan**. In Hong Kong, get an **Octopus card** at the MTR airport station on arrival.

🎟️
Book Ahead!
**Shanghai Disneyland** and **Hong Kong Disneyland** — book online well in advance. **Qiao Ai Lai Lai** — arrive early or book ahead. **The Chairman** (HK) — book 6–8 weeks ahead via thechairman.hk!

📱
Internet & VPN
China blocks Google, WhatsApp, Instagram etc. Install a **VPN before departure** — you cannot download VPN apps once inside China. Hotel WiFi is usually available. Hong Kong has full open internet.

🧻
Small Essentials
Carry a **small pack of tissues** — public restrooms in China may not have paper. Some tourist areas have squat toilets; bring hand sanitizer too. Hong Kong is more Western-standard.

🌡️
November Weather
**Shanghai:** Cool, 10–18°C, bring a jacket. **Shenzhen:** Mild, 18–24°C, light layers. **Hong Kong:** Similar to Shenzhen, 17–23°C, pleasant. Pack a mix of light and warm layers.

🦀
Peak Crab Season
**November is hairy crab season!** Order crab roe xiaolongbao at Qiao Ai Lai Lai without hesitation. Hairy crab is traditionally paired with Shaoxing wine and ginger vinegar. A must-eat moment on this trip.

🥢
Tipping
Tipping is **not customary** in mainland China — service charges are built in. In Hong Kong, 10% is appreciated at upscale restaurants if a service charge isn't already added.

These are instruments of extraordinary intention. Every component — the balance wheel oscillating at 21,600 times per hour, the Geneva stripes hand-applied to a movement plate, the jewel bearings that reduce friction to near-nothing — represents centuries of accumulated craft, passed from master to apprentice.

This collection is curated with that history in mind. Each piece is documented here not as inventory, but as testimony.
